Summary:
The dataset contains information about Spotify songs and user listening behavior. It includes details such as song name, artist, duration, popularity, and whether the song was skipped or played fully. The data helps in understanding user preferences and listening patterns.

Skip Rate Analysis:

Skip rate shows how often users skip songs before finishing them.

A high skip rate means users are not interested in certain songs.

Songs with low popularity or longer duration may have higher skip rates.

Popular, trending, or shorter songs usually have a lower skip rate.

High skip rate can indicate mismatch between user mood and song type.

Overall, skip rate helps identify which songs engage listeners and which fail to retain attention.
